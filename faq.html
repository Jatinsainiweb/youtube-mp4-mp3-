Frequently Asked Questions

Legal Questions

Is converting YouTube videos to MP3/MP4 legal?

Converting copyrighted content for personal use is generally permitted under fair use principles. However, distributing converted files or using them commercially without permission is prohibited.

Can I convert copyrighted music videos?

You may convert personal music videos for private listening, but redistribution or public performance requires proper licensing.

Technical Support

Why isn't my URL working?

Ensure you're using a valid YouTube URL in the format: https://youtube.com/watch?v=... We don't support age-restricted, private, or region-locked videos.

How long does conversion take?

Most conversions complete in 15-30 seconds depending on video length and server load. HD videos may take slightly longer.

Quality & Formats

What's the maximum quality for MP4?

We support up to 1080p HD resolution. Quality depends on the original video's available resolutions.

Do you offer lossless audio formats?

Currently we support MP3 up to 320kbps. FLAC/WAV formats are planned for future updates.

Device Compatibility

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! Our converter works perfectly on all smartphones and tablets through any modern web browser.

Do I need special software?

No installations required - our web-based converter works directly in your browser across all platforms.
