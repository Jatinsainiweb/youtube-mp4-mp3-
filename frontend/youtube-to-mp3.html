Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between quality settings?

Higher bitrates (320 kbps) provide better audio quality but larger file sizes. Lower bitrates (128 kbps) give smaller files but may have slightly reduced quality. Most users find 256 kbps to be an excellent balance.

How long does conversion take?

Most conversions complete within seconds. Longer videos or higher quality settings may take slightly more time.

Will I lose audio quality during conversion?

Our converter maintains the highest possible quality within your chosen bitrate. The final quality depends on the original video's audio quality and your selected bitrate.

Can I convert multiple videos at once?

Currently, we process one video at a time to ensure the best possible conversion quality for each file.

What devices support MP3 files?

MP3 is universally supported across all modern devices, including smartphones, tablets, computers, and music players.
