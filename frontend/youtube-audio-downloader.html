What's the maximum length of audio I can download?

Our downloader supports videos of any length, though longer videos will take more time to process.

We extract audio at the highest quality available from the source video. WAV provides lossless quality, while MP3 and M4A use high-quality compression.

Do I need special software to play the downloaded audio?
